Spicy Garlic Shrimp Pasta

A declicious and simple meal with a spicy kick

For those weekdays where you can't be bothered to spend an hours in the kitchen but are just as eager to avoid the cost of ordering out, this quick and easy meal can be prepared in 20 minutes or less!

It is delicious, cheap and made with only natural ingredients, which is perfect for those of us that are trying to eat healthy.

Ingredients

  • 400g dried pasta (I prefer spaghettini for this recipes)
  • 3-4 cloves of garlic (minced)
  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • Approximately 20 small shrimp (deveined and shell removed)
  • Arugula (a few handfuls will do)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • Oregano (to taste)
  • Red chili flakes (to taste)
  • Freshly grated parmesan

Steps

  1. Start off by adding a small amount of butter to pan on medium heat

    we can start boiling a large pot of water for the pasta while completing the following steps

  2. When the butter is completely melted, add the minced garlic, oregano and red chilli flakes to the pan
  3. Once the garlic starts to become aromatic, add the shrimp, salt and pepper and cook until the shrimp is pink throughout
  4. Remove the shrimp from the pan once it is fully cooked and add the rest of the butter
  5. Once the butter is completely melted we can add the arugla. Stir until it begins to wilt
  6. When the water reaches a rolling boil, add a generous amount of salt followed by the dried pasta cook the pasta until just before the time specified on the package is reached
  7. Finish cooking the pasta by adding it to the pan of arugla along with a half cup of the pasta water. Make sure to stir regularly
  8. Once the pasta is cooked, plate along with the shrimp we set aside earlier
  9. Finish off with freshly grated parmesan and enjoy!
